## Overview

Tumble Motion adds 2D physics simulation to After Effects, giving motion graphics artists a way to introduce more believable movement, tumble behavior, and force-based animation. It is intended for creators who want a dedicated tool that plugs into an After Effects workflow without forcing changes to the rest of their production pipeline.

The project is a good fit when your animation needs to feel more reactive and less rigid. With its focus on 2D physics for motion design, it supports workflows where pacing, movement logic, and composition all have to line up cleanly.
